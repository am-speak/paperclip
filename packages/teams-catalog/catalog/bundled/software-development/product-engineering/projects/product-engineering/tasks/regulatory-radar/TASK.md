Check 5 regulatory sources hourly for material changes affecting the company. For each source, determine whether there are new developments since the last check and assess impact on engineering operations, compliance posture, or product liability. The 5 sources are:

1. **EU AI Act** — European Commission AI Office publications and delegated acts
2. **GDPR & Data Protection** — EDPB decisions, guidelines, and enforcement news
3. **NIS2 / Cybersecurity** — ENISA advisories and national transposition updates
4. **Digital Services Act (DSA)** — Commission decisions, codes of practice, and platform regulation
5. **Software Liability / CRA** — Cyber Resilience Act developments and open-source liability rulings

If any source reveals a material change, create a blocked issue with a summary, link, and recommended action. If all sources are unchanged, post a short status comment confirming the radar sweep.
